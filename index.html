N-1 Contingency Screening + Wind Integration
Line 26 most critical at 157.5% loading · violations fall 77% at 80% wind

Automated N-1 contingency analysis on the IEEE 39-bus New England test network · the same methodology required by NERC TPL-001 for transmission planning at PJM, MISO, and CAISO. For each of 35 lines: remove from service, re-solve AC power flow via Newton-Raphson, check thermal and voltage violations, restore. Extended across five wind generation levels to show how renewable penetration affects grid reliability under single-element stress.
